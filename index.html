Our Story

Baked with Love, Served with Pride

Kamat Bakery is a cherished institution in Dharwad — a city famous for its culture, music, and now, its exceptional bread. We wake up before dawn so your morning begins with something extraordinary.

From our signature soft loaves to crunchy biscuits, cream rolls, and celebratory cakes, every item is made with hand-picked ingredients and a whole lot of heart.
